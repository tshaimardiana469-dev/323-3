About AVAPRO Financial Consulting

Our Company Background

Founded in 2011 in Exeter, United Kingdom, AVAPRO Financial Information Consulting was established to fill a critical gap in the market: independent, third-party financial advice that prioritizes client needs over product sales. What began as a small team of 5 financial analysts has grown into a respected firm with 28 professionals, serving over 500 clients across the UK and Europe.

We are regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and adhere to the highest standards of fiduciary duty, ensuring all our recommendations are in the best interest of our clients. Unlike traditional financial institutions, we do not receive commissions or kickbacks for promoting specific financial products, allowing us to maintain complete objectivity in our advice.

Our Exeter headquarters is strategically located in the heart of Devon's financial district, with satellite advisory points in London, Bristol, and Manchester to serve clients across England.

Our Financial Consulting Services

AVAPRO provides comprehensive tiered financial information consulting solutions designed to meet the unique demands of individual investors, small-to-medium enterprises, and institutional clients. Our services are categorized into core offerings (standard consulting) and premium solutions (custom, high-touch engagement), all backed by our rigorous research methodology.

Market Data Research

Core Service Real-Time Updates

Real-time global capital market data aggregation and analysis, including equity, fixed income, commodity, and forex markets. Our periodic industry trend reports include 12-month forward projections, while our macroeconomic indicator analysis covers inflation, interest rates, and geopolitical risk factors with monthly risk early warning notifications.

Deliverables: Weekly market briefings, monthly sector deep-dives, quarterly regional outlook reports, and ad-hoc trigger alerts for market-moving events.

Personal Wealth Planning

Core Service Customizable

Holistic personalized asset allocation schemes based on detailed risk tolerance assessment (including psychometric profiling and financial capacity analysis). We provide long-term wealth preservation consulting and low-risk investment portfolio suggestions aligned with your time horizon, liquidity needs, and financial goals.

Specializations: Retirement planning, tax-efficient investing, inheritance planning, and life-stage financial transition management (career change, marriage, retirement).

Institutional Financial Advisory

Premium Service Bespoke

Risk Control Consulting

Core Service Proactive

Comprehensive investment risk identification across asset classes, including market risk, credit risk, liquidity risk, and operational risk. Our asset volatility stress testing uses historical scenario analysis (2008 crisis, 2020 pandemic, 2022 rate hikes) to model portfolio resilience, with loss prevention strategy formulation and quarterly risk review sessions.

Tools: Custom risk scoring model, portfolio stress test dashboard, and early warning risk indicators tailored to your portfolio composition.

Financial Training & Briefing

Supplemental Interactive

Offline and online financial knowledge seminars hosted at our Exeter office (and virtually) for individual investors and corporate finance departments. Exclusive Custom Reports

Premium Service One-on-One

Deep-dive research reports developed through dedicated one-on-one collaboration, tailored to your specific industry, investment targets, and long-term financial goals. These reports include primary research (interviews, proprietary data collection) and secondary analysis (market data, regulatory reviews) with actionable recommendations and implementation roadmaps.

Typical deliverables: 50-100 page custom report, 2-hour presentation with our senior analysts, and 30-day follow-up consultation to address implementation questions.

Our Service Process

  1. Discovery Consultation: Initial 60-minute session to understand your financial situation, goals, and risk tolerance (free for new clients).
  2. Needs Assessment: Custom analysis of your current financial position and gap identification against your objectives.
  3. Proposal Development: Tailored service recommendation with clear deliverables, timeline, and pricing.
  4. Implementation Support: Hands-on guidance to execute recommended strategies (where applicable).
  5. Ongoing Review: Regular check-ins (quarterly/bi-annually) to adjust strategies as your situation or market conditions change.

Our process is collaborative – we encourage client input at every stage to ensure our recommendations align with your values and priorities.
